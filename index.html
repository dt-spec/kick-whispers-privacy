Privacy Policy

How Kick Whispers collects, uses, and protects your data.

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Kick Whispers is an unofficial companion extension for Kick and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or produced by Kick.

Section 01

Data We Collect

  • Kick account identity from OAuth (username / user ID)
  • User-generated direct messages
  • Conversation and app metadata needed for core functionality (including block settings and anti-abuse counters)
  • Billing metadata for paid subscriptions (Stripe customer / subscription identifiers), if billing is enabled
Section 02

How We Use Data

  • Deliver private messaging features
  • Store and sync conversation history
  • Prevent abuse and spam, enforce safety controls
  • Maintain account and session integrity
  • Manage subscription and billing status for Pro features
Section 03

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data
  • Data may be processed by required infrastructure providers (e.g., Firebase, Stripe)
  • If AI moderation is enabled, message text may be sent to the configured moderation provider for safety classification
  • Data may be disclosed when legally required
Section 04

Data Retention

  • Messages retained until deleted by user or removed by moderation or security policy
  • Technical logs and anti-abuse counters retained for limited operational periods
  • Billing metadata retained as needed for active accounts and legal or accounting obligations
Section 05

User Controls

  • Disconnect / logout at any time
  • Delete individual conversations
  • Block or unblock users
  • Manage billing in the Stripe portal (if enabled)
Section 06

Security

  • Data transmitted over secure channels in production
  • Secrets and credentials are not bundled in the extension package
