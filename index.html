Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Kick Whispers is a browser extension that adds a private messaging layer for Kick.com users. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Your Kick username and user identifier (via Kick OAuth)
  • Private messages you send and receive through Kick Whispers
  • Basic telemetry for abuse prevention (rate-limit counters, moderation signals)

How We Use Data

  • Deliver and display direct messages between opted-in users
  • Store conversation history for your reference
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized usage
  • Maintain secure account sessions

Data Storage

  • Message and conversation data is stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Session and anti-abuse data is stored on our backend service

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • Data is shared only with infrastructure providers required to run the service (e.g. Firebase)
  • Data may be disclosed if legally required

Data Retention

  • Messages are retained until deleted by you or removed under moderation/security policies
  • Technical logs and anti-abuse counters are retained for limited operational periods

Your Controls

  • Log out or disconnect the extension at any time
  • Delete individual conversations from the UI
  • Block or unblock users from messaging you

Security

All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections. Access credentials and service secrets are never bundled with the extension package.
