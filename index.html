Kick Whispers Privacy Policy

1) Last Updated

  • April 22, 2026

Kick Whispers is an unofficial companion extension for Kick and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or produced by Kick.

2) Data We Collect

  • Kick account identity from OAuth (username/user ID)
  • User-generated direct messages
  • Conversation/app metadata needed for core functionality (including block settings and anti-abuse counters)
  • Billing metadata for paid subscriptions (Stripe customer/subscription identifiers), if billing is enabled

3) How We Use Data

  • Deliver private messaging features
  • Store and sync conversation history
  • Prevent abuse/spam and enforce safety controls
  • Maintain account/session integrity
  • Manage subscription/billing status for Pro features

4) Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data
  • Data may be processed by required infrastructure providers (e.g., Firebase, Stripe)
  • If AI moderation is enabled, message text may be sent to the configured moderation provider for safety classification
  • Data may be disclosed when legally required

5) Data Retention

  • Messages retained until deleted by user or removed by moderation/security policy
  • Technical logs and anti-abuse counters are retained for limited operational periods
  • Billing metadata retained as needed for active accounts and legal/accounting obligations

6) User Controls

  • Disconnect/logout
  • Delete conversations
  • Block/unblock users
  • Manage billing in Stripe portal (if enabled)

7) Security

  • Data transmitted over secure channels in production
  • Secrets/credentials are not bundled in the extension package
