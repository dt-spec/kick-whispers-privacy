Moderation
How moderation
is handled

We actively moderate private messaging to protect users and platform health. Our system combines multiple layers of protection rather than relying on a single check.

We do not publish internal detection logic in detail to prevent abuse circumvention.

01
Verified access only Users must authenticate before using messaging.
02
Abuse prevention controls Rate limits, spam detection, and behavior controls help stop flooding and repeated abuse.
03
Automated safety screening Messages are evaluated for prohibited categories — scam links, violent threats, severe harassment, and other disallowed content.
04
Policy enforcement in real time Disallowed messages are blocked before normal delivery, and safety events are logged for operational review.
05
User controls Users can block and unblock others. Restricted users cannot message through those blocks.
06
Ongoing tuning Moderation rules and thresholds are reviewed and adjusted as safety needs evolve.
Mechanics
How private messages work

One-to-one DMs between users who opt in and authenticate their account.

Delivery Model

Messages are sent in real time through the Kick Whispers backend — not through native Kick whisper APIs.

Who Can Message

Only authenticated, opted-in users in the system. Blocks are enforced at the delivery layer.

Safety Before Delivery

Messages pass moderation and anti-abuse checks before final delivery — nothing reaches your inbox unscreened.

Tier History Behavior
Free  ↔  Free

Runtime / temporary history — not designed as permanent lifetime storage.

Any chat with Pro

Conversation history is persisted for long-term access. Upgrading to Pro removes Free messaging limits and enables persistent history for all Pro-involved chats.

What to include

A good bug report gets fixed faster. Try to include:

  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • What you expected vs. what actually occurred
  • Your Chrome version and OS
  • Whether it happens every time or only sometimes
  • Any error messages or unusual behavior you noticed
